What is Gardening: underground?
Gardening underground is the newest way to take part in a movement towards fresh locally grown food. Gardening underground is more than a marketplace for produce, its social media for growers.
how to grow .
when you sign up for a gardening underground you can choose to be a "grower" or an "E-grower" growers fill an overall profile about thheir garden and techniques like if they grow organic, hydroponic etc. then fill out sub profiles on their crops, it will be easy to update pictures through the app. Once a crop starts making ripe produce, the grower can post "daily yeild" photos for each crop once every 24hrs. this photo will be verified by "e-growers"
By looking at the photo and entering the number of produce in the picture, checking if they are ripe, and giving and opinion on how they look on a scale of 1 to 10. each photo will be verified by X number of E-growers and the consensus between them will be checked against what the grower entered in the post. the other option for E-growers is to mine the GUG coin transactions.
how to buy/sell
once produce has been grown, uploaded, and verified by E growers, it is ready to be sold. Growers will be able to mark their "market" location and the hours which they can meet there, these will be locations like the local park, drug store, or even a grocery store! we are asking that people do not use their home addresses and will not display addresses but a general area where the grower can meet. Growers will also be able to post any public farmers markets they will be vending at.
while GUG coin might be available on an exchange, we reccomend growing even small amounts of veggie in your closet
